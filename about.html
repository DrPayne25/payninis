Payninins How it all Started

Alex and Quentin grew up both having a love of all meals regardless of time of day. "I mean who doesn't right." When fate happened to partner these two up for their first group project in CodeFellows 102 you already know it would incorporate the love both of them share in meals! Payninis was born in that moment a box lunch company that caters to all 3 meals of the day. Now you might be asking "Quentin & Alex are you monsters that hate Brunch?" Why no dear reader while Payninis is not currently offering Brunch we do have future plans to include this due to our mutual love of the meal but, Payninis can share with you that we are working on getting our WA state liquor license so that we can offer the most important item during brunch mimosas!!!! Until this is done Q and I have decided that we can't in good faith offer this option until we acquire that thanks for you patience and understanding.

Sincerely,
Alex & Quentin
